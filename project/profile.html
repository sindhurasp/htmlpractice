Indian Oscar Winners 2023

95th Academy Awards held in Los Angeles and India grabbed 2 Oscars for Best Original Song and Best Documentary category. Out of three nominations, India made it won 2 Academic Awards at 95th Academy Awards Function. M.M. Keeravani and Chandrabose brings home Oscar Award for 'Naatu Naatu' song in SS Rajamouli's 'RRR'. Guneet Monga has won the Best Documentary Short category award for 'The Elephant Whisperers'. Shaunak Sen Aman Mann was nominated for Best Documentary Feature for “All That Breathes”.
